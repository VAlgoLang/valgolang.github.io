Introduction¶

Welcome to Manim DSL’s documentation!

Manim DSL is a Domain Specific Language that is made to help lecturers make animations for teaching data structures and algorithms

Here is an example:

let stack: Stack = new Stack;
stack.push(5);
stack.pop();
